THE HISTORY OF HOTEL KP

The distinctive architecture of the Hotel KP offers a blend of classy contemporary and modern features. The spacious must-visit lobby, the grand staircase, ballroom, mezzanine, Veranda, the downstairs Grill Room, and all the public areas are a masterful work credited to a Dallas native James Williams. He is just one of Texas premier designers of luxury Hotels.

Construction began on The Hotel KP in 2004 with the help of the Hotel KP Board members, proudly led a civic campaign to raise $3,000,000 in financing through the sale of stock. The Hotel KP has quickly established itself as a favorite for people needing to take a break and be pampered or if you need a venue to host a party/wedding/meeting. In September 2009 Hotel KP added a Banquet Room, (2) Ballrooms, and (4) Board Rooms as part of a 18,000 sqft addition to the Hotel KP.

THE TIMELINE OF HOTEL KP

Planning The Planning for Hotel KP began in February 2003 with a small committee of 4 people.
Finalize Planning On New Years day 2004 it was official that Hotel KP would be built and open later the same year.
Grand Opening In September 2004 Hotel KP opened it's doors for business, with a full guest listing until the end of year.
Hotel KP Additions August 2009 Hotel KP had added an additional 18,000 sqft of amenities and parking.
