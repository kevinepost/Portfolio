About Hotel KP

Our property is home to a spa, numerous outlet shops, several restaurants and lounges. And that's on top of the 228 guest rooms, which visitors describe as spacious and comfortable with plush bedding and marble bathrooms that include soothing whirlpool tubs.

Travelers also say that there's no shortage of things to do at this resort, from practicing your golf swing to lounging by one of the four outdoor pools. However, if you plan on doing any sightseeing in Dallas or Fort Worth area, we provide free limousine service to and from your location as part of your stay with us. When you stay with us, we just want you to relax and let us take care of you!
